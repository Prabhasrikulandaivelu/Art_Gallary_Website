Why I Draw?

Drawing helps me to reduce stress and calms my anxiety.

If I begin a drawing feeling agitated, grumpy, and stressed out, I always end a drawing feeling more relaxed, content, and at peace. I draw the stress. I draw my negative thoughts. Sometimes literally. And as the negativity leaves my pen, it leaves me. This is my stress release.
